So, what are the advantages and disadvantages of this mango quality detector


Hello everyone, let's understand more deeply what Fruit Counting Mango and Quality Inspection is, we can abbreviate it as mango quality detection. Well, this detection tool is used to help farmers who have mango plantations because with so many mango trees at harvest time the workers are not very careful in checking the mangoes, whether they are fresh or not, so some consumers feel disadvantaged because they bought semi-fresh mangoes. or close to rotten. So the advantage of this mango quality detection tool is that we can find out and differentiate which mangoes are healthy and suitable for sale to consumers and which mangoes are rotten and not suitable for sale.

The downside is that this mango detection tool does not always work as it should. It is necessary to take appropriate and not blurry pictures of the mango so that this detection tool can work properly and this tool also does not always produce accurate results.
